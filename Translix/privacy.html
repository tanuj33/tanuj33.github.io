Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Translix is a privacy-first tool. No data ever leaves your machine.

1. Data Collection

Translix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or the contents of the files you process. All localization logic is performed locally on your device.

2. File Access

The app only accesses the specific CSV files and folders you explicitly select. It does not have background access to your file system.

3. Third Parties

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. Your translation keys and values remain your intellectual property and never touch our servers.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact tarunchaturveditechnical@gmail.com.
