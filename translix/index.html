Why Translix?

Localizing an iOS app into 10+ languages usually means hours of manual entry. Translix reduces that to seconds. By acting as a bridge between your spreadsheets and Xcode, it ensures your translation keys are always in sync.

Walkthrough

  1. Export: Save your Google Sheet or Excel as a .csv.
  2. Import: Drag and drop the file into Translix.
  3. Magic: The tool parses the columns and maps them to the correct language ISO codes.
  4. Finalize: Direct export into your Xcode project folder.
