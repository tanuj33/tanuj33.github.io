SpeakSend Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-03

1. Introduction

SpeakSend is an iOS app that lets you record voice notes, convert them to text, and receive the transcription by email. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

SpeakSend is published under the tanuj33.in domain by Tanuj Sharma (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

2. Information we collect

2.1 Audio recordings

When you use SpeakSend, your audio recording is sent securely to our servers or directly to a third-party transcription provider so that we can convert speech to text. We do not use your audio for advertising or model training.

2.2 Transcriptions

The text transcription generated from your audio is used to:

  • Display the transcript inside the app, and
  • Send the transcript to the email address you provide.

2.3 Email address

We collect your email address so we can deliver the transcript to your inbox and, where you consent, send important notices about the app (such as service issues or changes to this policy).

2.4 Usage data

We may collect basic, aggregated analytics such as the number of recordings, approximate duration, and error logs. This helps us keep the service reliable and improve performance. We do not build advertising profiles based on this data.

3. How we use your information
  • To process your audio and generate a text transcription.
  • To send transcripts to the email address you provide.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the SpeakSend app.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations, if required.
4. Third-party services

SpeakSend uses trusted third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Speech-to-text provider – for converting your audio to text. Your audio and transcription are processed according to their terms and privacy policy.
  • Email delivery provider – for sending transcripts to your inbox.
  • Apple – for app distribution and in-app subscriptions via the App Store.

We only share the minimum data required for these services to work (for example, the audio to the transcription provider, and your email plus transcript to the email provider).

5. Data retention

Audio recordings and transcriptions may be stored for a limited time to ensure reliable delivery and troubleshooting. We aim to retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law.

If you would like to request deletion of your data associated with SpeakSend, you can contact us using the support email below.

6. Children’s privacy

SpeakSend is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your choices
  • You can choose not to use the app if you do not agree with this policy.
  • You may request access to, or deletion of, data we hold about you by emailing us at support@tanuj33.in.
  • You can unsubscribe from any optional emails by using the link provided in the email or by contacting us.
8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be communicated inside the app or via email.

10. Contact
