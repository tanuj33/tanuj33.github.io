SpeakSend Support

Need help with the app, subscriptions, or your transcripts? You can reach out using the details below.

Contact

For all support requests related to SpeakSend:

Email: support@tanuj33.in

When you contact us, please include (if possible):
• Your device model and iOS version
• The version of SpeakSend you are using
• A short description of the issue and steps to reproduce it
• Any error messages shown on screen

Subscriptions & billing

SpeakSend uses Apple’s in-app subscriptions. We do not have access to your payment details and cannot cancel or refund purchases directly.

To manage your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your name at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select SpeakSend to change or cancel your plan.
Data & privacy questions

If you have questions about how your data is used, or want to request deletion of data associated with your use of SpeakSend, please email us at support@tanuj33.in.

For full details, see the SpeakSend Privacy Policy.
