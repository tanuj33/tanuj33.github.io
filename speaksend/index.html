Frequently asked questions

What happens if I run out of minutes?

You’ll see a prompt inside the app. You can either wait for your monthly allowance to reset on your next billing date, or buy a one-time extra minutes pack (for example 50, 100, or 200 minutes) using Apple’s in-app purchase. These top-ups do not auto-renew.

Do I need an account to use SpeakSend?

No separate login is required. Your subscription and purchases are handled through your Apple ID.

Where do my transcripts go?

After transcription, your text is shown in the app and emailed to the address you configure. You can search, forward, or archive them just like any other email.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Go to Settings > Your name > Subscriptions on your iPhone, select SpeakSend, and change or cancel your plan.

How is my data handled?

We only collect the minimum data needed to transcribe your audio and send your emails. For details, see the SpeakSend Privacy Policy.
